# 書目與論文清單

> **這頁只列引用，不放內容。**內容在 `01-` 開頭的檔案。
> 拿到一本書/一篇 paper 想驗證，來這裡找來源。

## 一手原典

### 書

- **Chess, S. & Thomas, A. (1977).** *Temperament and Development.* Brunner/Mazel.
  - 完整版九維度框架的提出。把 1960s NYLS 數據整合成實用框架
- **Thomas, A., Chess, S., Birch, H.G., Hertzig, M.E. & Korn, S. (1963).** *Behavioral Individuality in Early Childhood.* NYU Press.
  - NYLS 原始研究方法 + 早期發現
- **Thomas, A. & Chess, S. (1987).** *Temperament and Development.* Brunner/Mazel.（修訂版）
  - 加上 1980s 對「難養型」的後續追蹤

### 重要 paper

- **Chess, S. (1974).** "Temperamental differences: a critical concept in child health care." *Pediatric Annals.*
  - 應用篇：把氣質概念帶進兒科實務
- **Thomas, A. & Chess, S. (1977).** "Genesis and evolution of behavioral disorders: from infancy to early adult life." *Journal of the American Academy of Child Psychiatry.*
  - NYLS 早期到成年的追蹤報告
- **NYLS Young Adult Period Report**（見 PubMed 標題 "The New York Longitudinal Study (NYLS): the young adult periods"）
  - NYLS 最終階段（年輕成人期）的收尾報告

## 後續擴充理論（待補到 `02-後續理論擴充與修正.md`）

- **Rothbart, M.K. (1981).** "Measurement of temperament in infancy." *International Journal of Behavioral Development.*
  - 提出 IBQ（Infant Behavior Questionnaire）家長填寫量表
- **Rothbart, M.K. & Derryberry, D. (1981).** "Development of individual differences in temperament." In Lamb & Brown (Eds.), *Advances in Developmental Psychology.*
- **Rothbart, M.K., Ahadi, S.A., Hershey, K.L. & Fisher, P. (2001).** "Investigations of temperament at three to seven years: the Children's Behavior Questionnaire." *Child Development.*
  - 把 IBQ 擴充到學齡前，CBQ 問卷
- **Kagan, J. (1994).** *Galen's Prophecy: Temperament in Human Nature.* Basic Books.
  - 提出「behavioral inhibition」——大約 10-15% 嬰兒屬高度反應型，跟膽小/焦慮相關
- **Buss, A.H. & Plomin, R. (1984).** *Temperament: Early Developing Personality Traits.* Erlbaum.
  - EAS 模型（Emotionality, Activity, Sociability），三維度簡化版
- **Cloninger, C.R. (1987).** "A systematic method for clinical description and classification of personality variants." *Archives of General Psychiatry.*
  - 七因子氣質性格模型

## 跨文化 / 神經生物 / 應用研究（待補到 `03-實證研究綜述.md`）

- **Kohnstamm, G.A., Halverson, C.F., Havill, V.L. & Mervielde, I. (Eds.). (1995).** *Portraits of Temperament in Early Childhood.* Guilford.
  - 跨文化研究，比較 NYLS 在不同國家是否成立
- **Fox, N.A., Henderson, H.A., Pérez-Edgar, K. & Pine, D.S. (2005).** "Behavioral inhibition: linking biology and behavior within a developmental framework." *Annual Review of Psychology.*
  - 神經生物學（杏仁核、前額葉）與 behavioral inhibition 的連結
- **Posner, M.I., Rothbart, M.K., Sheese, B.E. & Voelker, P. (2012).** "Control networks and linkages between emotion and cognition." *Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences.*
  - 氣質與注意力/執行功能網絡的關聯

## 應用 / goodness-of-fit（待補到 `04-應用：氣質與教養適配.md`）

- **Chess, S. & Thomas, A. (1991).** "Goodness of fit: Clinical applications." In Strelau & Plomin (Eds.), *Individual Differences in Children.*
- **Lerner, J.V. & Lerner, R.M. (1983).** "Temperament and adaptation across life." In *Life-span Development and Behavior* (Vol. 5).
- **Keogh, B.K. (2003).** "Temperament in the Classroom." Brookes Publishing.
  - 學校/班級應用
